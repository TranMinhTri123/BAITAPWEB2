E-Commerce

E-commerce is the purchase and exchange of goods and services over computer networks (such as the internet) through which transactions or terms of sale are performed electronically.

ECommerce requirements
Processing gateway Digital Certificate
Shopping cart Merchant account

Ecommerce can be broken into four main categories

  • B2B (Business-to-Business)
  • B2C (Business-to-Consumer)
  • C2B (Consumer-to-Business)
  • C2C (Consumer-to-Consumer)
